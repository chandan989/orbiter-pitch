[ HOW IT WORKS ]

The Launch Sequence

A five-stage protocol to achieve a stable, verifiable orbit for your digital assets.

1. Pre-Flight

Connect wallet and provide the domain. Our system performs initial compatibility checks.

2. Authorization

Prove ownership by adding a unique TXT record to your domain's DNS settings.

3. Valuation

Our protocol calculates the asset's objective market value based on key metrics.

4. Tokenization

Customize on-chain parameters like share symbol and total supply.

5. Mission Complete

Your asset achieves stable orbit as a tradable Aptos Object.
